ecosystem

Use Spectrum to own a % of our NFT portfolio, earn, buy, sell and swap NFTs

Features

Spectrum marketplace

Spectrum’s Portal is the epicenter of the Spectrum ecosystem. Users will be able to perform multiple functions through it. Spectrum marketplace will give you the opportunity to create, buy, swap, sell your art and share it with the community. We are making NFTs more approachable and accessible. You will find the newest and hottest NFT trends in our marketplace. NFTs from famous high-profile trendsetters all in one place.

Spectrum NFT Portfolio

By holding Spectrum token you own a % of our NFT portfolio. Profits will be redistributed amongst holders proportionally

NFT giveaways

Every week, Spectrum holders get a ticket to participate in our giveaway and can earn NFTs.

Exclusive deals

The project aims at giving exclusive access to sales and early access to projects we believe in.

Upcoming Marketplace

At the end of every week, users can stake their tokens to vote for an artwork. On sunday, the artists fund is split amongst the artists based on the votes they got. By voting and staking, users get rewards.

Weekly contests

At the end of every week, users can stake their tokens to vote for an artwork. On sunday, the artists fund is split amongst the artists based on the votes they got. By voting and staking, users get rewards.

Join the artists’ pool”

Anyone can apply to become an artist but first they will need to get 30 votes from the community, they also need to hold a min. of tokens to be eligible.

Tax distribution

There is a 10% tax on every transaction

3%

of every transaction (buy, sell or swap) will be added to the artists fund. We will use those funds to buy and trade NFTs and in the future reward artists.

2%

of every transaction is re-distributed evenly to all holders. This means that the amount of tokens in your wallet increases automatically over time.

2%

of every transaction is automatically added to the liquidity pool. Liquidity will automatically grow over time as the token is traded.

3%

of each transaction would be added to Marketing and Development. It is key and being able to have growing marketing funds would help us in the long run.
