Upcoming Marketplace

Spectrum’s Portal is the epicenter of the Spectrum ecosystem. Users will be able to perform multiple functions through it. Spectrum marketplace will give you the opportunity to create, buy, swap, sell your art and share it with the community. We are making NFTs more approachable and accessible. Features

Spectrum NFT Portfolio

By holding the token you own a % of our NFT portfolio. Profits will be redistributed amongst holders proportionally.

NFT Launchpad

NFT projects incubator allowing projects to raise capital. We'll offer fair and accessible fundraising for NFT artists.

Staking

You can stake your tokens to vote for your favorite artworks. By voting and staking users get rewards.

Artist's Fund

At the end of every week, users can stake their tokens to vote for an artwork. On sunday, the artists fund is split amongst the artists based on the votes they got. By voting and staking, users get rewards.

Join the artist’s pool

Anyone can apply to become an artist but first they will need to get 30 votes from the community, they also need to hold a min. of tokens to be eligible.

Roadmap

While Spectrum is in early stages, we're convinced in the project's ability to grow organically overrun, building trust through transparent governance. The project's structure and future are built on our belief in the power of Blockchain.

Our long-term goal for Spectrum is to develop a solution that expands the utility of NFTs beyond collectibles and art by adding value to users. As we scale and escalate, we pledge to include the community as a partner in the success of the project, with opportunities to participate in governance at every stage.

Tax distribution

There is a 10% tax on every transaction:

3%

of every transaction (buy, sell or swap) will be added to the artists fund. We will use those funds to buy and trade NFTs and in the future reward artists.

2%

of every transaction is re-distributed evenly to all holders. This means that the amount of tokens in your wallet increases automatically over time.

2%

of every transaction is automatically added to the liquidity pool. Liquidity will automatically grow over time as the token is traded.

3%

of each transaction goes to Marketing and Development. It is key and being able to have growing marketing funds would help us in the long run.
